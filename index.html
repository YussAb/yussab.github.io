Problems

Problems worth solving

These are examples of themes and opportunities I find interesting. This section is meant to start conversations with founders, operators, investors, and collaborators.

01

Cognitive Health in the Age of AI

As screen time and use of AI increase, people are experiencing more difficulty focusing, remembering information, and managing mental fatigue. However, there are still very few tools that help individuals understand and control how technology affects their cognitive health, creating a growing and largely unmet need.

02

The Healthy Living Gap

Maintaining a healthy lifestyle requires sustained effort, yet daily routines, limited time, and competing priorities often prevent people from consistently adopting health-supportive behaviors. While awareness of healthy habits is widespread, there are still limited systems and tools that effectively support long-term behavior change, resulting in a persistent gap between intention and action and leading to avoidable long-term health issues.

03

The Hidden Value of Older Adults

Older adults represent a growing and often overlooked resource in society. They possess valuable experience, knowledge, and, in many cases, available time. However, there are limited opportunities and systems that enable them to actively contribute and create value, leading to a significant underutilization of their potential.
